## About Me

Dongan Tan is a PhD candidate at Texas Tech University, specializing in International Relations and Public Administration, with a minor in Research Methods. Her research explores the dynamics of economic sanctions in the context of globalization, utilizing a range of methodologies, including game theory, causal inference, network analysis, and so on. Her work has been published or is forthcoming in peer-reviewed journals, including the _International Journal of Social Welfare_. She also teaches courses in Political Analysis and International Relations, where she is passionate about inspiring students to achieve their full potential.
